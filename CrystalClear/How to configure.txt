Set an option to OFF to disable it, ON to enable.
Leave it at NOTSET to let the game run normally.

Suggested OFF:
Grain
Dithering
Vignette
Bloom
UIBloom
Grunge
Scanlines

Settings also include TurnBanner which speeds up combat considerably
by disabling the banner overlay and the delay that accompanies it.

You can also disable the dust storms that obscure your view in combat.
